Whether you are a teenager in high school looking at whether to go Xbox or PlayStation, or a parent wanting to give their kid the best Christmas gift ever. The debate on consoles can be a tricky one. Whether someone who loves video games yourself, or you know someone in your life who does, finding the right console can be a foreboding task. The goal of this website is to list possible pros and cons of the three main current generation videogame consoles: Xbox series X, PlayStation 5, and Nintendo Switch.

Keep in mind that opinions stated here a just that, opinions. The pricing for the PlayStation 5 is about the same as the Xbox Series X. Please note, I will be focusing on differences between the PlayStation 5 and the Xbox Series X more prevalently. Something also to note is that all three major consoles currently have a “cheaper” version, in the case of the PlayStation and Xbox, they have two versions you can choose from, the “Standard” edition is what you would normally expect from a videogame console, being able to put game disks inside to play your games. But a cheaper “Digital” version is changed, and the disk drive and all the components needed to read your game disks are taken out. Saving you around $100 for the PlayStation and closer to $200 on the Xbox, with some major drawbacks though.
