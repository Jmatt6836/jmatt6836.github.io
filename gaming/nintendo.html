Nintendo was one of the first gaming companies to ever exist, they created one of the first at home gaming consoles and have been in the running for best console ever since. But what Nintendo does that is different than the other two companies is that Nintendo knows that the only place you will get their games is through them. If you want to play Mario Bros, Mario Kart, Super Smash Brothers, Kirby, Pokémon, Animal Crossing, and more, you must do it through them. So, Nintendo has an interesting position. They aren’t fighting against the other companies. All they need to do is to get you to want one of their games. Most people, I included, who have consoles have a PlayStation or Xbox and a Nintendo Switch. While not being the most frugal option, it gives you the most games possible without having all three. Some other Pros and cons of the Switch are things like portability. The Nintendo switch is the only one of the three that is completely portable while also being able to serve as a home console. A first of its kind, and while not possessing the best battery life. It isn’t competing against anyone else for that. It also misses out on a lot of the “Cross-Compatible” games, games like the Call of Duty franchise that makes games for both other consoles. In the end, while missing out on some mainline titles that both other companies have access too. Nintendo has some of the most popular exclusive games on the market and keeps its standing in the “Console Wars”.
