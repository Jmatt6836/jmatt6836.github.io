White Space and Clean Design

Walmart.com

Walmart Homepage

Walmart's use of white space allows it to show enough information without making it either feel too crowded or like there isnt enough space.

Visual Heirarchy

Nintendo.com

Nintendo Homepage

Nintendo uses Visual Heirarchy to show off their new game and have us focus on it. It is much larger than anything else on the screen.

Contrast

Amazon.com

Amazon Homepage

Amazon's use of contrasting colors makes the user feel comfortable and easy going. The colors are soft and not harsh, allowing for an easy shopping experience.
