Wedding Services!

Here at Temple Inn and Suites, we want to help you make sure your most magical day stays magical! So to do that we offer:

  • A reception hall that can be modified to meet your needs!

  • Tables, chairs, and all other needs for your group up to 300 people!

  • For a fee we can set up and take everything down for you! Don't let that slow down your perfect day!

  • An in house catering service that can help you feed all of your loving patrons, or if you have someone catering, they can have access to our ovens for heating and our serving area!

  • A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving
